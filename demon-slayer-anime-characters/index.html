Anime Characters

Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is a Demon Slayer who primarily uses Water Breathing but also masters Sun Breathing (Hinokami Kagura), a powerful, inherited family breathing style.

Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko Kamado is the deuteragonist of the Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba series. She is the younger sister of the protagonist, Tanjiro Kamado.Nezuko was transformed into a demon by Muzan Kibutsuji

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma is a Demon Slayer who uses the Thunder Breathing style, which focuses on fast and powerful attacks. He later develops the unique Seventh Form, Flaming Thunder God, proving his mastery and solidifying his status as one of the strongest slayers.He is friend of both nezuko, Tanjiro.

Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke is a Demon Slayer who uses a self-taught breathing style called Beast Breathing, which he created after being raised by boars. This style is characterized by his feral and aggressive fighting, use of two serrated swords, and heightened sense of touch to detect opponents through air vibrations.He is friend of tanjiro, zenitsu and nezuko.

Kyōjurō Rengoku

Kyojuro Rengoku is a central supporting character in Demon Slayer, known as the Flame Hashira. He is a kind, cheerful, and honorable swordsman with a strong sense of justice, who lives by his mother's teachings to protect the weak. He is a powerful demon slayer who specializes in the Flame Breathing technique and played a major role in the Mugen Train Arc.

Giyu Tomioka

Giyu Tomioka is a Demon Slayer and the Water Hashira, who uses the Water Breathing style. This is a mastery of swordsmanship that allows him to perform powerful, water-based attacks and techniques.

Shinobu Kochō

Shinobu Kocho is a demon slayer known as the Insect Hashira, who uses the unique Insect Breathing style, a derivative of Water Breathing. Since she lacks the physical strength to decapitate demons, she developed this style to compensate by focusing on agility and speed, using a stinger-like sword to inject a potent wisteria-based poison instead of brute force.

Kanao Tsuyuri

She is a Demon Slayer, the adoptive sister of Kanae Kocho and Shinobu Kocho and Shinobu's former Tsuguko.Kanao Tsuyuri uses the Flower Breathing style, which is a derivative of Water Breathing and was passed down to her by the former Flower Hashira, Kanae Kocho.

Obanai Iguro

Obanai Iguro is a Demon Slayer and the Serpent Hashira who uses a custom breathing style called Serpent Breathing. Developed from the Water Breathing style, Serpent Breathing is a sneaky and unpredictable technique that mimics the slithering movements of a snake. His mouth is covered by bandages and is accompanied by his snake. Kimetsu AcademyKyojuro Rengoku.

Mitsuri Kanroji

Mitsuri Kanroji is a Demon Slayer who uses the unique "Love Breathing" style, which she developed from Flame Breathing. This style is uniquely suited to her extraordinary flexibility and high muscle density, which allows her to perform acrobatic, whip-like slashes with her specialized blade. Her cheerful nature goes hand in hand with her role as the Love Hashira.

muzan kibutsuji

"Muzan Kibutsuji is the main antagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, known as the Demon King and the progenitor of all demons. Originally a human born in Japan's Heian Period, he was turned into a demon by an experimental medicine intended to cure him of a fatal illness. His goals are to achieve immortality and conquer the sun by either finding the rare blue spider lily or creating a demon that can. He is incredibly powerful, ruthless, and cowardly, often using other demons to do his bidding while avoiding direct confrontation unless necessary. He is the one who killed the tanjiro's Family"

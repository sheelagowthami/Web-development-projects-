THE VISION BEHIND HERITAGELENS

Why Heritage Needs
an Intelligent Voice

Thousands of years of human achievement stand in stone and marble across India. Most visitors walk past it without understanding a word of its story.

🔴 The Problem

India has over 3,500 protected monuments and 42 UNESCO World Heritage Sites — yet most visitors experience them as beautiful ruins without context.

Information is locked in academic books, behind expensive guided tours, or buried in Wikipedia pages few people read.

Young digital natives lack an interactive way to connect with cultural heritage.

🟢 The Solution

HeritageLens AI acts as an intelligent companion that explains history in simple, human language.

It combines AI chat, voice narration, visuals, and location awareness to bring monuments alive.

Just type a monument name — or point the Lens — and the story begins instantly.

🌟 Our Vision

Everyone should be able to understand any monument instantly, regardless of language or education level.

This project is a step toward making cultural knowledge universal and interactive.

🎯 Who It's For

Students — learn history interactively.

Tourists — understand places deeply.

Researchers — quick cultural reference.

Everyone — curiosity-driven exploration.

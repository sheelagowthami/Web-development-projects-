About HeritageLens AI

An AI-powered platform that brings historical monuments to life

🚀 What is this project?

HeritageLens AI is a smart tourism assistant that uses AI simulation, computer vision concepts, and interactive UI to help users explore historical monuments in an engaging way.

🎯 Mission

To make heritage learning interactive, AI-driven, and accessible for everyone.

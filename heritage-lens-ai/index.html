Featured Heritage Sites

Discover India's most iconic monuments with AI-powered explanations

Taj Mahal

Agra, Uttar Pradesh | 17th Century

The iconic white marble mausoleum, built by Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Red Fort

Delhi | 17th Century

Historic fortified palace built by Shah Jahan. Red sandstone walls symbolize Mughal power.

Charminar

Hyderabad, Telangana | 16th Century

Iconic structure with four minarets built in 1591. Symbol of Hyderabad.

Hampi

Karnataka | 14th-16th Century

Sacred center of Vijayanagara Empire with stunning temple ruins. UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Qutub Minar

Delhi | 12th-13th Century

Tallest brick minaret at 72.5m built by Qutb-ud-din Aibak. UNESCO World Heritage Site.

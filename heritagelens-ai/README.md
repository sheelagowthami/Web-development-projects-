HeritageLens AI
Preserving Heritage Through AI-Powered Storytelling
HeritageLens AI is a Smart Tourism and Heritage Technology project designed to make cultural heritage more accessible, interactive, and educational using artificial intelligence.
Problem
Historical monuments and cultural sites often have information that is difficult to access, understand, or engage with, especially for younger generations and tourists.
Solution
HeritageLens AI transforms heritage information into AI-powered storytelling experiences, providing simplified historical explanations, cultural insights, multilingual accessibility, and tourism recommendations.
Features
AI-powered heritage storytelling
Cultural knowledge exploration
Multilingual accessibility
Tourism recommendations
Educational learning experience
